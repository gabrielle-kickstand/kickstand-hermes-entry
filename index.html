The Challenge

Manychat was beloved by creators but barely known to the wider B2B marketing world. Two months before the flagship summit, the project hit a double disruption: a new primary client contact joined mid-stream, and the original Hollywood venue became unavailable just 14 days before doors were set to open. For an event built on the theme of real human connection, a logistics failure would have been far more than a scheduling headache.

The Strategy

Kickstand stepped in as the strategic backbone — not just a vendor. We pivoted full production to 1 Hotel West Hollywood in 14 days while preserving every element of the premium, invite-only feel. In parallel, we built continuity with the new client lead, coordinated with top-tier creators to make their presence substantive rather than ceremonial, and drove the "Conversations, Not Posts" media narrative from the ground up.

The Impact

The event sold out, hit 100% capacity, and generated 10+ major industry placements that shifted Manychat's narrative from "Instagram tool" to "AI-powered commerce leader." The human side of the story was just as strong — the event built lasting trust with the new client lead and drew public praise from industry peers for the team's exceptional relationship-building.
