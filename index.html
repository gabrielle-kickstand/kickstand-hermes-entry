The Challenge

Manychat was beloved by creators but barely known to the wider B2B marketing world. With a flagship summit on the horizon and a last-minute venue change already underway, they needed more than logistics support — they needed a PR and communications partner who could fill seats, generate buzz, and make sure the right people showed up.

The Strategy

Kickstand stepped in as the PR and communications lead — not as event planners, but as the team that made sure every seat was filled and every headline was earned. We worked in close coordination with the event production team, activated creator networks, drove attendance through strategic outreach, and built the "Conversations, Not Posts" media narrative from the ground up.

The Impact

The event sold out, hit 100% capacity, and generated 10+ major industry placements that shifted Manychat's narrative from "Instagram tool" to "AI-powered commerce leader." The numbers were the result of a targeted PR strategy — strategic media outreach, creator activation, and communications that made the event unmissable.

T-60 Days

New Client Lead, Same Momentum

A new primary contact joined Manychat's side of the partnership just two months before the event. Kickstand quickly built trust, aligned on strategy, and ensured the transition added energy rather than friction to an already packed planning process.

T-14 Days

The Venue Crisis

The original Hollywood venue became unavailable just 14 days out. While the event production team handled the venue transition, Kickstand activated immediately — standing up a rapid media and communications response to keep attendees informed and excitement high.

The Pivot

14 Days. New Venue. Zero Compromise.

The event team executed a full venue transition to 1 Hotel West Hollywood. Kickstand's role: keep the story positive, keep press engaged, and keep the attendance pipeline full. No panic in the headlines — just anticipation for what was shaping up to be an unmissable event.

Event Day

400+ Industry Leaders. One Room. Full House.

Creators, marketers, and brand owners from across the ecosystem gathered for a day of breakthrough conversations and real-world case studies. Featured talent including Zach King brought star power that made the Manychat story land at a whole new scale.
